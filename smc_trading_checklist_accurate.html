1) Hard Filter: Market Context

  • Start on 1H or 4H. Structure first, entry tool second.
  • Bias is clear: trending, or a confirmed CHoCH has already shifted bias.
  • I know the external swing high/low that matters.
  • I am not trading random internal chop.
If structure is unclear, there is no trade.

2) Premium / Discount Filter

  • Dealing range is drawn from the relevant external swing low to high.
  • Buy only in discount (below 50%).
  • Sell only in premium (above 50%).
  • I am not buying high or selling low in the middle of the range.

3) Valid HTF POI

Order Block checklist

  • The OB aligns with a meaningful BOS or CHoCH.
  • The move away showed displacement / urgency.
  • The OB is still fresh / unmitigated.
  • The OB has extra confluence: nearby liquidity and/or FVG.

FVG checklist

  • The FVG formed from a move that caused a meaningful external BOS or CHoCH.
  • The FVG is with trend or with a confirmed reversal.
  • The FVG is still unmitigated.

4) Liquidity Story

  • Liquidity is marked clearly: equal highs/lows, prior session high/low, swing points.
  • I know what price is likely to sweep before the move.
  • I know what liquidity I am targeting after entry.
  • The target pool is large enough to justify the risk.

5) Lower-Timeframe Confirmation

  • Price reached the HTF POI first.
  • A liquidity sweep occurred inside or just before the zone.
  • A valid 5M CHoCH / MSS / BOS formed after the sweep.
  • The displacement leg created a new 5M OB and/or FVG.
  • I am waiting for retrace into the execution zone, not chasing the impulse candle.

6) Entry Model

TypeWhen to useEntry
ConfirmationDefault modelRetest of 5M OB / FVG after sweep + CHoCH/BOS
RefinedVery clean setupMidpoint / 50% of impulse, OB midpoint, or FVG midpoint
AggressiveOnly strong confluenceDirect touch of HTF POI with predefined invalidation
Default choice: Confirmation. Aggressive entries are lower quality unless the confluence is exceptional.

7) Stop Loss Rules

  • SL goes beyond invalidation, not at a random tight distance.
  • For buys: below the sweep low or below the OB/FVG invalidation.
  • For sells: above the sweep high or above the OB/FVG invalidation.
  • If stop is too wide, I reduce size or skip the trade.

8) Take Profit Rules

  • TP1 = nearest internal liquidity / first logical reaction point.
  • TP2 = structural high/low or external liquidity.
  • Exit plan is defined before entry.
  • Minimum planned R:R is acceptable for the setup type.
SetupMinimum planned R:R
Confirmation1:2 preferred, 1:3 ideal
Refined1:3 preferred

9) Session / Timing Filter

  • Prefer high-volume windows: London and New York for forex-style SMC execution.
  • Avoid forcing entries in low-volume chop or right into major news.
  • If trading crypto/indices, adapt timing to the market's active session instead of copying forex times blindly.

10) Automatic No-Trade Conditions

  • No clear HTF bias.
  • POI already mitigated.
  • FVG did not cause external BOS/CHoCH.
  • OB has no displacement / no intent.
  • No sweep.
  • No lower-timeframe shift.
  • R:R below plan.
  • Trade is driven by FOMO.
